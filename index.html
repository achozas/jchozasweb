📋 Product Description

The Universal Adaptive Gripper is an innovative assistive device designed for people with reduced hand mobility. Its purpose is to facilitate the manipulation of everyday utensils such as spoons, forks, pencils and other small objects.

Combining ergonomics, functionality and simplicity, this gripper allows users to perform daily tasks with greater independence and comfort, significantly improving their quality of life.

📖 How to Use the Gripper

  1. Grip: Hold the device by the thickened area with your available hand
  2. Insertion: Place the utensil (spoon, fork, pencil) in the front slot
  3. Fixation: The utensil is secured in the gripper's slot
  4. Use: Now you can use the utensil with full control, as if holding it directly
  5. Stability: The lower base keeps the gripper stable on the table
🎯 Ideal for:
  • Eating (spoons, forks, knives)
  • Writing and drawing (pencils, pens)
  • Tasks requiring precision
  • Daily activities with one hand

⚙️ Technical Specifications

Dimensions and Geometry:

  • Total length: 120 mm
  • Cylindrical handle: 80 mm × 35 mm (diameter)
  • Grip area: 40 mm (diameter)
  • Insertion slot: 25 mm × 10 mm (width × depth)
  • Stabilizing base: 50 mm × 50 mm × 10 mm

CAD Components (5+ shapes):

  • ✓ Horizontal cylinder (main handle)
  • ✓ Thick cylinder (ergonomic grip area)
  • ✓ Cone (smooth transition between sections)
  • ✓ Rectangular slot (boolean subtraction for insertion)
  • ✓ Base cube (stabilization)

Suggested Materials:
